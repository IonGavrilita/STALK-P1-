About

Welcome to STALK, your number one source for all things stocks. We're dedicated to giving you the very best market reference tool, with a focus on company info, financial news, and market trends. Founded in 2020 by four University of Washington's coding bootcamp students, STALK has come a long way from its beginnings. When the STALK team first started out, their passion for market analysis drove them to do tons of research, so that STALK can offer you the world's most advanced supplemental market analysis tools. We now serve customers all over the country, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. we hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, The STALK Team Lili, Jaime, Ion, Cam
